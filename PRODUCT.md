# Product

## Register

product

## Users

An operator uses Tweet Lab as a private content-drafting cockpit: reviewing prior posts, source material, business context, live X inspiration, and drafts, then editing and scheduling without publishing until explicitly approved. This Agent-Native build adds a first-class agent that acts inside the app through the same actions as the UI.

## Product Purpose

Turn an operator's approved context sources, X voice DNA, inspiration accounts, and live X signal into grounded private drafts through Hermes, then edit and schedule them through Postiz. Success means opening the app, generating from real signal, refining in the compose panel, and scheduling only after explicit approval.

## Brand Personality

Refined, operator-grade, sharp, deliberate. Calm and premium, not loud or gimmicky. A pro instrument you live inside, not a toy.

## Anti-references

Avoid AI slop: generic gradient dashboards, glassmorphism-by-default, neon excess, hero-metric template, identical card grids, eyebrow-on-every-section, fake metrics, decorative motion with no purpose.

## Design Principles

1. Receipts over vibes: show what real signal fed a draft (and what's missing) honestly.
2. Editorial restraint: composed spacing, typography, and surface contrast; legible first.
3. Private by default: draft-only until the operator approves; scheduling is explicit.
4. Dense but breathable: fast access to controls with real visual rhythm.
5. Motion is feedback, not decoration: every animation communicates state, hierarchy, or arrival.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast for body/controls, visible focus states, keyboard-reachable, reduced-motion-safe transitions, no content gated behind animation.
